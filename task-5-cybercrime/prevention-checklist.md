Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify the identity of the sender before making transactions
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Use strong passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Keep your banking apps updated regularly
- Do not respond to unknown calls asking for personal details
- Report fraud immediately at cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline 1930 in case of fraud.
